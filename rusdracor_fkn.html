Корпуса драмы в TEI

  • Théâtre Classique: 1090 plays from the 17th and 18th century
  • Shakespeare His Contemporaries: 860 plays written between 1550 and 1700
  • German Drama Corpus: 471 German-language plays from 1730 till 1930
  • Letteratura teatrale nella Biblioteca italiana: 171 Italian plays
  • Russian Drama Corpus: 144 Russian-language plays from 1720s till 1940s
  • Dramawebben: 62 Swedish plays
